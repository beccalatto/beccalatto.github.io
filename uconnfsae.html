ABOUT US

  • University of Connecticut's School of Engineering offers many opportunities for students looking to participate in extracurricular activities. However, few offer the prestige and hands-on experience that is given to the members of UConn Formula Society of Automotive Engineers (FSAE). UConn Formula SAE is a student run organization with the goal of designing, manufacturing, and racing a Formula-Style car competitively.
The Team meets every Tuesday at 6:30PM and Saturday at noon. Our shop is located in Depot Campus at 71 Romano Rd Storrs CT, 06269. We pick up anyone who needs a ride at the Lodewick Visitors Center around 6:00PM.
